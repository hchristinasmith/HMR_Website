About HRM Pet Food

At HRM Pet Food Company, we believe that every pet deserves the best nutrition possible. Our products are made with carefully selected ingredients to ensure optimal health and happiness for your furry friends.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, HRM Pet Food Company started with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality pet nutrition using New Zealand's finest ingredients. Our team of veterinary nutritionists and food scientists work together to create perfectly balanced formulas for cats and dogs of all ages.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Using only high-quality, locally sourced ingredients
  • Maintaining strict quality control standards
  • Supporting pet health through optimal nutrition
  • Environmental sustainability in our production processes

Quality Assurance

Every batch of our pet food undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards for nutrition and safety. We are proud to be certified by New Zealand's leading pet food safety organizations.
